Best Practices Learned:
- Use at least 12-16 characters.
- Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
- Avoid dictionary words and personal info.
- Use passphrases for better memorability.
- Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA).

Common Password Attacks:
- Brute Force: Trying all possible combinations until correct.
- Dictionary Attack: Using common words and known passwords.
- Phishing: Tricking users into revealing passwords.
